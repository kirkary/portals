Portals Master Privacy Policy

Last updated 28 September 2016

This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used and disclosed by Vitalii Maslikov when you use the Portals Master mobile application (the “Game”).

Vitalii Maslikov reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at reasonable times, so please review it frequently. Your continued use of Game will signify your acceptance of the changes to this Privacy Policy.

Collection and Use of Personal Information

Game uses many third party SDKs. These SDKs are used for advertising to our users, analyzing service usage, enabling in-app purchases, or providing game services like achievements and leaderboards. We allow third party operators to collect information below solely for internal operations of the service as permitted by applicable rules and laws. This information may include your IP address, operating system("OS"), browser type, domain names, access times, and referring website addresses, device ID (persistent/non-persistent), hardware type, media access control (“MAC”) address, international mobile equipment identity (“IMEI”) and your location (based on your IP address).

Non personal information is general, non-identifiable and anonymous information that is gathered for statistical, analytical and research purposes such as the improvement of Game.

Children Age 13 and Under

Game does not knowingly collect or solicit personally identifiable information from children under the age of thirteen.

Game may use social networks (Facebook, Twitter and etc.) to allow users to have fun by exchanging their game scores with other users over the age of 13.

We rely on the age restrictions from these networks to make sure only users over the age of 13 can share information.

Malware/Spyware/Viruses

Game does not knowingly permit the use of malicious software such as malware and spyware.
